Welcome to the Virtual Hospital

In the midst of COVID-19, patients suffering from various injuries or dieseases are unable to visit the hospitals due lock down or just to protect themselves from corona.Hence, we have developed a website wherein the patients can get treatment from the specialists from the comforts of their homes.The appointment is booked online, a link is sent to the patient through an SMS. The patient dials the doctor at the appointed time, get the check up done and doctor uploads the prescription with her electronic signature. This platform also provides a COVID-19 detector.All you have to do is to upload the image of the x-ray of your lungs.
